Installation
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Copy and paste into the 'Trainset' folder of the MSTS or Open Rails route where you intend to use these.

UKSteamTrack - Part 1 Bogie Coaches Enhanced Sound is required.


The Real Thing
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Information is provided separately.

Carriages are modelled (as far as possible!) as running in the late 1930s.
