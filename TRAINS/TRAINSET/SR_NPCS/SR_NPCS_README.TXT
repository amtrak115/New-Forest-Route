Installation
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Copy and paste into the 'Trainset' folder of the MSTS or Open Rails route where you intend to use these.

UKSteamTrack - Part 1 Bogie Coaches Enhanced Sound and Part 3 - 4 Wheeled Short Wheel Base Wagon Sounds are required.    


The Real Thing
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

A separate list of vehicles is provided together with information about their operation.

Carriages are modelled (as far as possible!) as running in the late 1930s.
